Scenario 3: Cooldown + Time Sensitive Toggle — Same Customer, Same Day

Customer Anita received an RCS message from Trends at 10 AM today. She's now in 24-hour cooldown on RCS. JioMart wants to send her a flash sale campaign at 2 PM. What happens?
⚡ Time Sensitive = ON (Flash Sale)
Anita is excluded from the campaign. She will not receive the flash sale message — not now, not later. The sale ends tonight and a delayed message would be meaningless.

Reachability panel shows: "1 customer excluded — in cooldown (Time Sensitive)"
📬 Time Sensitive = OFF (New Collection)
Anita is queued. Her message is held until tomorrow 10 AM when cooldown lifts. Before delivery, the system re-checks her caps — if still valid, the message goes out.

Reachability panel shows: "1 customer queued — will be delivered after cooldown"

Pre-Send Validation Flow — What Happens at Dispatch

Step 1: DND / Opt-Out Check
Is the customer DND-registered or opted out of this channel?
Pass → Continue
Fail → Suppress (DND_OPT_OUT)
Step 2: Channel Availability
Does the customer have this channel? (e.g., RCS-capable device)
Pass → Continue
Fail → Suppress (CHANNEL_UNAVAILABLE)
Step 3: Channel Cap Check
Has the customer hit 4/28d on this channel from this format?
Pass → Continue
Fail → Suppress (CHANNEL_CAP_EXCEEDED)
Step 4: Global Weekly Cap Check
Has the customer received 7 promotional messages in the last 7 days?
Pass → Continue
Fail → Suppress (GLOBAL_CAP_EXCEEDED)
Step 5: Cooldown Check
Is the customer currently in cooldown on this channel?
Not in cooldown → Dispatch ✓
In cooldown → Check Time Sensitive toggle ↓
Time Sensitive ON → Suppress (COOLDOWN_TIME_SENSITIVE)
Time Sensitive OFF → Queue for later + re-validate
Step 6: Dispatch ✓
All checks passed. Message delivered. Customer enters cooldown on this channel.
